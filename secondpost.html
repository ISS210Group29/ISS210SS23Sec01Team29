Amnesty International

Posted by Max Fenster on April 28, 2023

Amnesty International is a global organization that focuses on human rights issues. They have been a strong supporter of firearm regulation in the United States since 1961. Their article on gun violence is the one I read for my part in this project and I learned that:

  • There were 316 incidents of gunfire at schools from 2013-2018
  • 2%-7% of all pediatric trauma injuries are gun related
  • 1,814 children died in incidents involving firearms in 2018
  • March For Our Lives, a movement started by the Parkland High School shooting survivors, has been working since the shooting to promote new gun legislation, lobbying to congress for stricter laws, and even raises money for victims of gun violence
  • Change starts with state governments, creating stricter background checks, more regulations and guidelines before we can start to see change on a national level
  • Another new guideline is mandatory training for anybody that is interested in purchasing a firearm, and a required license reneweal every few years
  • Gun reform WORKS! Many countries in Western Europe, Asia, Australia, and even New Zealand have implented very strict laws regarding firearms which have given them some of the lowest gun violence rates in the world
